Why Another Dating App?
Most dating apps feel more like a game than a way to actually meet someone. You spend hours scrolling through profiles, starting chats that go nowhere, and wondering if anyone on the other side is even real. Then come the paywalls. You have to subscribe just to see who liked you or to send a message. It starts to feel less like you're trying to find a real connection and more like you're stuck in a system designed to keep you paying and alone. It's exhausting, and we got tired of it. #somethingbetter.

So We Created InPrson Dates.
We built InPrson for people who are tired of all that. No subscriptions, no pay-to-unlock features, and no endless swiping. Just one simple identity check to make sure everyone is real, and then it’s free. Forever. We’re focused on helping people meet face-to-face, have real conversations, and maybe even build something lasting. That’s it. No tricks, no distractions, just dating the way it should be. #dateInPrson.

Our Mission
Dating used to be simple. You met someone, you talked, you felt something real. That’s what we want to bring back. We’re not here to trap you in endless swipes or monthly fees. We’re here to create a space where people are honest, present, and serious about connection. Our goal is simple: to help you go on real dates. Because that’s the only way to truly know if you’re compatible. It’s the only way to feel the vibe, read their energy, and see if something meaningful is there. Texting is fine, but it’s no substitute for showing up in person. More InPrson dates. More lasting relationships. That’s the kind of love we believe in, and the kind we’re here to build.

Together We Make This Work.
InPrson is free to use and always will be. No subscriptions, no hidden features, no upsells. Instead of charging you to message or match, we ask for a one-time identity verification fee of $18.37 once your account is approved. This helps keep the community safe, respectful, and full of real people looking for real relationships. That small fee covers the cost of secure third-party verification and helps support the platform. It’s not about profit, it’s about protecting the experience for everyone. After that, everything is open. Always.
