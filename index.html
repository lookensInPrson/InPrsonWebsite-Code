Meet InPrson. Go On Dates. Get Married
Dating has changed a lot over the years. Somewhere along the way it got more complicated. More apps, more swiping, more texting. But less real connection. We want to bring things back to what matters most, meeting someone in person and seeing where it leads. At InPrson, we believe that meaningful relationships start face to face. It’s about sharing a moment, feeling the energy, and being present. That kind of connection is hard to find through a screen. Our goal is simple. To help more people go on real dates. Because that’s when things get real, and that’s the kind of love we’re here to help you find.

Free Now, Free Forever.
InPrson is free to use. No subscriptions. No paywalls. No gimmicks. The only required cost is a one-time identity verification fee of $18.37, charged after your account is approved. This step helps keep the community safe by filtering out fake profiles and making sure everyone is here for the right reasons. It covers secure third-party verification and protects the experience for everyone. We don’t run ads, and we don’t sell your data. We don’t believe in turning your love life into a revenue stream. Instead, we rely on this small fee and the support of people who believe in what we’re building. If InPrson means something to you and you want to help us grow, you’ll have the option to contribute through voluntary donations. No pressure, just real support from a community that cares.
